Salesforce Live

Live visual mixing
Screen content management

I contribute actively to Salesforce Live, the flagship event organized by Salesforce Belgium, with production support provided by Blue Moon. This annual gathering serves as a vital platform for innovation, collaboration, and connection within the Salesforce community.

During this event, I manage videomixing operations across multiple setups, including an advanced 3-projector blend and a multi-LED wall configuration to deliver seamless and captivating visual experiences. After the main keynotes, attendees participate in three simultaneous workshops using silent disco headphones, where I efficiently coordinate all sessions in parallel.

Being part of this dynamic event on a yearly basis is an incredible experience. I look forward to continuing to bring value and create impactful experiences at every edition of Salesforce Live.

Master Control Room Operator at VRT

Through my work, I help VRT maintain its reputation for delivering outstanding content to viewers nationwide.

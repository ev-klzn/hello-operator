  • Pixelhue P20-DS

    Description

    The Pixelhue P20 is a high-performance 4K presentation switcher designed for demanding live events, broadcast environments, and large-scale AV productions. Featuring a fully modular architecture, the P20 supports up to 12 inputs and 8 outputs , offering incredible flexibility to adapt to a wide range of setups. With true 4K@60Hz processing and ultra-low latency, it ensures stunning visual fidelity and smooth, real-time switching. The P20 excels in seamless transitions, advanced scaling, and multi-layer processing, making it ideal for dynamic screen layouts and complex visual environments.

    Its robust feature set includes support for multiple PGM/PVW outputs, EDID management, and custom resolutions—perfect for LED walls, projection mapping, or hybrid broadcast workflows. The system also includes intuitive control options via touchscreen interface, software control, or third-party protocols. Operators can manage multiple layers, keying effects, and source routing with precision and ease. Whether it's a live concert, corporate event, or televised production, the P20 offers professional-grade power and flexibility. Add the Pixelhue P20 to your rental lineup to elevate your video production to the next level.

    • Modular I/O Design with 4K Processing. The P20 features a modular architecture supporting up to 12 inputs and 8 outputs, configurable with a range of card types. It handles true 4K@60Hz 10-bit 4:4:4 video processing, delivering exceptional image clarity and color depth. This makes it ideal for high-resolution LED walls, projection, and broadcast setups. Multi-format input support (HDMI, SDI, DisplayPort) ensures compatibility across a wide range of sources.
    • Advanced Layer Management and Seamless Switching. Operators can work with multiple layers per output, each with adjustable position, size, and keying options. Seamless switching with ultra-low latency ensures smooth transitions, perfect for live productions where timing and precision are critical. Custom resolutions and scaling options allow for dynamic screen configurations, including unconventional aspect ratios. Integrated EDID management and auto signal detection streamline the setup process.
    • Flexible Control and Real-Time Monitoring. Control the P20 via its built-in touchscreen, Windows-based control software, or third-party protocols like TCP/IP. Real-time preview and program monitoring outputs make it easy to manage content before it hits the screen. The system is designed to integrate smoothly into complex workflows, from touring shows to permanent installations. Its compact, road-ready build offers reliability without sacrificing power.
    • P20-DS Model with SDI Outputs and Dante over IP. The P20-DS variant enhances the platform with native SDI output capability, ideal for broadcast applications. It also supports audio and control routing over IP via Dante, enabling seamless integration into modern networked AV environments. This allows for flexible, scalable system design—especially in venues or workflows using Dante-based audio infrastructure. The added connectivity options make the P20-DS even more versatile for hybrid and distributed production setups.
